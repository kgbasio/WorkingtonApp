Dometic Roll Out Awning

Home

  • Rolling The Awning Out

    • Step 1

      Unlock the travel clips

    • Step 2

      Loosen the arm tension clamps

    • Step 3

      Switch the awning drum to roll out. This switch is found at the end of the awning drum closes to the rear of the van

    • Step 4

      Use the awning strap to roll the awning out until it has reached the desired position

    • Step 5

      Slide the awning rafter arm from the bottom of the main awning arm to the top of the main awning arm. Locking in above the rafter arm lock in clip

    • Step 6

      Using the main awning arm release lever, extend the main awning arm to the desired length

    • Step 7

      Tighten the rafter arm tension clamp

  • Rolling The Awning In

    • Step 1

      Loosen the rafter arm tension clamp

    • Step 2

      Using the main awning arm release lever, lower the main awning arm down to the seating pin

    • Step 3

      Push on the awning rafter locking clip at the top of the main arm to release the awning rafter arm. Slide the rafter arm all the way to the base of the main arm

    • Step 4

      Maintaining downward pressure on the awning drum, switch the awning drum to roll up. Slowly guide the awning up to the travel position. The awning will have significant force and will want to roll up. It must be guided up slowly

    • Step 5

      Tighten the rafter arm tension clamps

    • Step 6

      Lock the inner and outer arms together with the travel clips
